## Description

**Extract stunning color palettes from any photo in seconds.**

PaletteSnap is the ultimate color extraction tool for designers, artists, and anyone who loves color. Simply snap a photo or pick one from your library, and PaletteSnap instantly identifies the dominant colors using advanced color analysis.

**KEY FEATURES:**

🎨 **Smart Color Extraction**
Capture 5-8 dominant colors from any photo using advanced k-means clustering algorithm. Get accurate, beautiful palettes every time.

🔢 **Multiple Color Formats**
View colors in HEX, RGB, and HSL — tap any value to copy it to your clipboard instantly.

🎯 **Color Harmony**
Explore 8 types of color harmony suggestions: complementary, analogous, triadic, split-complementary, tetradic, monochromatic, shades, and tints.

✏️ **Fine-Tune Colors**
Long-press any extracted color to manually adjust it with a precise color picker.

📁 **Organize & Save**
Save unlimited palettes, name them, and organize into folders. Search through your collection effortlessly.

📤 **Beautiful Exports**
Export your palettes as stunning 1080x1920 cards — perfect for sharing on Instagram, Pinterest, or with your design team.

📸 **Camera & Library**
Extract colors from a live camera shot or choose from your photo library.

🌗 **Dark Mode**
Full dark mode support for comfortable use day and night.
